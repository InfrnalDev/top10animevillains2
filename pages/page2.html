The Top 10 Anime Villains Of All Time (Page 2)

  • Ryomen Sukuna

    • The living embodiment of all that is cursed energy Sukuna is the ultimate evil. Nothing within him is good save for the person he inhabits. Will Yuji Itadori overcome the will of Sukuna? We shall see.

  • Muzan

    • After becoming immortal due a curse which turned him into a demon Muzan was forced to spend eternity within the shadows. No longer able to stand it Muzan wages war against the Demon Slayer Corps in hopes of finding a way to return to the light of day.

  • Perfect Cell

    • The result of Dr. Gero's inhumane experiments. Perfect Cell is the perfect weapon of mass destruction. What more need a supervillain be? Did I mention he is nearly indestructible?
