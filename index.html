The Top 10 Anime Villans Of All Time

  • Madara Uchiha

    • Raised a honorable shinobi he originally had great promise as a leader. However blinded by power the great Madara unfortunately lost his way. Fortunately in his old age however he was able to regain what was lost in the end. His humanity.

  • Capt. Aizen

    • The once prolific Capt. Aizen met his defeat at the hands of his own avarice for power. Once heralded as a shining example of a shikigami the now disgrace Capt will spend a millenia pondering his crimes.

  • Frieza

    • The infamously ruthless destroyer of planets. The great lord Frieza met his match at the mighty hands of the Z fighter numerous times yet somehow the evil lord is always able to find a way to seek retribution.
